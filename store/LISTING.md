## Full description (max 4000)

```
Spaced helps you actually remember what you study, using the two most
evidence-backed learning techniques there are: active recall and spaced
repetition.

Instead of re-reading notes, you pull each answer from memory and grade
yourself. Spaced then schedules every item to come back right before you'd
forget it — day 1, 3, 7, 14, 30, 60, 120 — so weak cards return often and
strong ones fade away. You spend your time only on what you're about to lose.

Everything stays on your device. No account, no sign-up, no ads, no tracking,
and it works completely offline.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

• Build flashcard decks fast — paste a whole list and Spaced splits each line
  into front and back, or add cards one at a time.
• Per-card scheduling — every card is tracked on its own spaced-repetition
  timeline, so you stop seeing the ones you already know.
• Study your way — flip cards, type your answer for auto-checking, reverse the
  deck (answer → prompt), shuffle, or drill only your starred "hard" cards.
• See exactly what's due today on a clean home screen, with your streak,
  recall rate, and mastery at a glance.
• Turn any topic into a study hub — attach notes, links, files, and images
  alongside its deck.
• Focus sessions — a built-in study timer with an optional active-recall
  prompt at the end, so you review while it's fresh.
• Smart reminders — get a notification the day a topic is due (and when a
  focus session ends), scheduled locally on your device.
• New-card pacing — new cards are introduced gradually (up to 10 per hour) so
  a big deck never overwhelms you.
• Backup and restore — export everything to a single file and bring it to a
  new phone whenever you like.

WHO IT'S FOR

Students, language learners, med and law students, developers memorizing APIs,
anyone studying for an exam or certification — or anyone who's tired of
forgetting what they just learned.

WHY SPACED REPETITION WORKS

Your memory fades on a predictable curve. Reviewing an item just as it's about
to slip resets that curve and makes the memory last longer each time. Do this a
handful of times and a fact moves into long-term memory for good. Spaced
automates the timing so you don't have to think about it — you just show up and
recall.

Private by design. Your study data never leaves your device unless you export
it yourself.
```
