# Privacy Policy — Spaced

_Last updated: 21 June 2026_

Spaced ("the app") is a study app for active recall and spaced repetition. This
policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: **everything stays
on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your data.**

## What the app stores

All of the following is stored **locally on your device only**, in the app's
private storage:

- Topics, notes, study material, links, and flashcards you create
- Your review history, schedule, streaks, and statistics
- Focus-session records
- Voice-recall audio recordings you make
- Files and images you attach to topics
- App settings (reminder time, reminder on/off)

The app has no account system and no backend server. None of this data leaves
your device unless **you** explicitly export it (see below).

## Data we collect

**None.** Spaced does not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports,
advertising identifiers, or any personal information. There are no third-party
trackers or ad networks in the app.

## Network use

Spaced works fully offline. It does not send your study data anywhere.

## Permissions the app requests

- **Notifications** — to remind you when a topic is due for review and when a
  focus session ends. Reminders are scheduled locally on your device.
- **Microphone** — only when you choose to record a spoken "recall out loud"
  answer. Recordings are saved locally and never uploaded.
- **Storage / files** — to let you attach files or images to a topic and to
  export or restore a backup file.

You can deny or revoke any of these in your device settings; the rest of the app
continues to work.

## Exporting and sharing

The app includes a backup feature. When you tap **Export backup**, the app
creates a JSON file of your data and hands it to your device's share sheet. What
happens to that file afterwards (saving to Drive, emailing it, etc.) is entirely
your choice and is governed by whichever service you send it to. Spaced itself
never transmits the file.

## Children's privacy

The app does not collect any data and is suitable for all ages. It does not
knowingly gather information from anyone, including children.
